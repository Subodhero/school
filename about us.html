About our school

The culturally diverse area where Gorkha Secondary School is located makes it a shining example of holistic education. We provide a broad curriculum with a committed faculty that gives students knowledge and character. Our state-of-the-art facilities, which include labs and a well-stocked library, encourage participatory learning. Beyond the classroom, we support participation in extracurricular activities that foster leadership and social responsibility. To mold individual potential, we think a strong parent-school connection is essential. Our school, which has its roots in the community, combines local values and modern technology to create citizens of the world. Students at Gorkha Secondary School embark on a transformative journey where they embrace a variety of opportunities and pave the way for a bright future for both themselves and their community.
